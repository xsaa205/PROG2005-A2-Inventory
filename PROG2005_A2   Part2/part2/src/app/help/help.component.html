Help & FAQ

How do I add a new item?

Go to Manage Items page, fill in the form (ID must be unique, all fields except comment required), and click Add.

How do I edit an existing item?

Click the Edit button next to the item in the table. The form will be filled with its data. Modify and click Update.

How do I delete an item?

Click Delete next to the item. A confirmation dialog will appear to prevent accidental deletion.

Why does data disappear after refreshing the page?

This application does not use a backend database. Data is stored only in memory. For persistent storage, you can implement localStorage (optional).

How do I search for an item?

Go to the Search page, type a keyword (partial match) and click Search.

What are "Popular Items"?

Items marked with Popular = Yes appear in the popular section on the Manage Items page.
